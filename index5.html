Breaking Barriers, Shaping Trends

Celebrating the Influence and Creativity of Women in Design

Women play a pivotal role, in enriching the field of design with diverse perspectives, creativity, and innovation. Their unique insights and talents challenge traditional norms, profoundly shaping visual communication.

Elizabeth Friedländer

One of the first women to design a typeface, her work for the Bauer Type Foundry and Penguin Books exemplified precision, elegance, and resilience in the face of adversity.

Jessica Hische

Illustrator and lettering artist whose typographic craft has shaped modern design across books, brands, and digital interfaces.

Paula Scher

A leading figure in graphic design and branding, known for bold typography and iconic visual systems for clients like MoMA and Citibank.

April Greiman

A pioneer of digital design and New Wave typography, Greiman fused technology and visual expression to redefine the graphic design landscape of the 1980s.
